Conference Program

Details will appear soon. The official program will take place entirely on Friday, 20 November, with talks held over the course of the day, beginning in the morning. It will consist of several longer invited lectures, followed by shorter contributed talks, with the goal of providing anyone interested in speaking with the opportunity to do so.

We plan to hold a dinner for all interested participants on Friday evening, after the talks have concluded.
